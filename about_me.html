Biography

My name's Sean Henderson. I'm 20 years old and I'm from Severna Park, Maryland. Severna Park, if you're not familiar (likely), is a town not far from Annapolis! These days I find myself in Windsor Mill, Maryland, which is in close proximity to the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC), where I go to school. At UMBC, I'm a junior. I'm slated to graduate in the Fall of 2023!

I have a great number of hobbies that keep me occupied. I love animals-- particularly ferrets. You'll be seeing mine a bit on this site, if my image weren't an indicator enough of this. I also have a dog named Mason, who's a mini goldendoodle. Additionally, I love the outdoors, particularly in the fall. Hiking and kayaking make for a great day outside! I collect vinyl records, too. I have a liking of both classic rock and EDM, but I'll really listen to whatever sounds nice.

My Journey with Computers

I love computers, and have since I was very little. I still remember tinkering with my family's PC which ran Windows XP, learning all that I could about the system and how to use it. My passion grew with time, as building computers became one of my biggest hobbies. Now, I am a full-fledged computer scientist! Though,I still have a soft spot for that old family PC and Windows XP-- especially Space Cadet 3D Pinball. If you've played it, you know what I'm talking about.

I am proud of how far I've come. Through my studies, I have become proficient in Python, C++, Java, HTML (including CSS and JS), C#, SQL, and R. Additionally, I have a working knowledge of both C and the NASM x86 and MIPS assembly languages. Software that I know well includes Discord, Visual Studio Code, Visual Studio, MySQL Workbench, Jupyter Notebook, WinSCP, Filezilla, PuTTY, TeamViewer, Wireshark, and more.
